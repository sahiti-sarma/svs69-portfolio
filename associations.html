ASSOCIATIONS

Active Minds

Active Minds is a nonprofit organization focused on promoting mental health awareness and education among young adults, particularly on college campuses.

UXPA

The User Experience Professionals Association (UXPA) is an organization that supports individuals involved in researching, designing, and evaluating user experiences for various products and services.

Interaction Design Association (IxDA)

The Interaction Design Association (IxDA) focuses on improving the human condition by advancing the discipline of interaction design.

International Association for Accessibility Professionals (IAAP)

The IAAP is dedicated to defining, promoting, and improving the accessibility profession globally through networking, education, and certification.
