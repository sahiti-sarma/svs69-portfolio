Hello! My name is Sahiti

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm in the New Jersey/New York areas, blending academic expertise with practical skills. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry with a minor in Psychology from the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign. Now, I'm a grad student at Drexel University, studying Human-Computer Interaction and User Experience.
My journey from science to UX design gives me a fresh perspective. Chemistry taught me to analyze complex problems, while psychology gave me insights into user needs and motivations. In my work, I bridge the gap between technology and people. I create intuitive interfaces and prioritize inclusivity and accessibility in digital spaces. Driven by curiosity and a passion for impactful experiences, I'm always looking to innovate in UX design and collaborate with others to make a positive impact.
